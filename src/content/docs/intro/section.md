## Getting Started

1. **Drop Cards**: Use `bdrop (bd)` to drop 2 random cards, claim a card by tapping the number button.
2. **View Collection**: Type `bcollection (bc)` to see the new card in your collection. Continue collecting more cards to receive more EXP and rewards!
